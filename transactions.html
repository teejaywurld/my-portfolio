Overview

This case study highlights the importance of user-centered design in improving a Mobile's effectiveness and achieving financial goals. This savings platform is a financial tool that allows users to deposit and save their money while earning interest on their savings. It is a convenient and secure way to save money for various financial goals, such as emergencies, future expenses, or long-term investments. The primary focus here is to encourage the users to save and provide them with the flexibility of withdrawing when needed.

Design Challenge

With the increasing complexity of personal finances and the evolving needs of individuals, there is a lack of user-friendly and technologically advanced savings platforms that offer a seamless and comprehensive solution for efficient money management, goal tracking, and maximizing savings potential. Building a savings platform that addresses these challenges would provide individuals with a user-friendly, intuitive, and technologically advanced solution to effectively manage their finances, improve savings habits, track progress towards goals, and enhance their financial well-being, that is where HAG savings platform comes in.



Design Approach

In creating a successful financial app, it is important to balance aesthetics and functionality while ensuring that the designs are accessible, providing affordances that address user needs. I achieved this by combining intuitive navigation, high-quality visuals, and personalized and goal-oriented features. While creating these designs, I drew inspiration from similar products like Sumo trust app, Kuda app, carrying out design processes like, using brainstorming techniques, user flows, user journey maps, and other ideation methods to explore different possibilities. The colors were carefully selected to provide the feeling of Calmness, Security and Reliability

Low Fidelity Designs

As part of my design process I created low-fidelity designs to quickly visualize and communicate ideas. Focus on representing key features and functionalities rather than perfecting details.



High Fidelity Designs

The design for this savings mobile app focuses on the user experience of prospective customers. A registered user would be prompted to login, in order to gain full experience and control of what happens within the app. Email and Password are the media through which a user can login to HAG 301 Savings platform, but as a new unregistered user, more information such as: Service number, address, fullname, etc. would be required to create an account on the app.


Registering/Creating an account as a new user, you would be prompted to choose who to register as, Personnel or Civilian/Retiree.

Dashboard and Other Features

On the Dashboard, are some other features where users can make payments, perform a quick savings action, terminate their account, amongst other features

Uploading documents like payment receipt made offline, to the savings app, for record purposes



Loan Request Feature

This feature gives a user the flexibility to request for loan within their payment capacity. There are ranges and categories of available loans with its duration and respective interest rate.
