Manage all tasks and earn rewards

Overview

I-Do to-do app is a task management app that is designed to help users stay motivated and productive by incentivizing task completion with monetary incentives. Some of the features of I-Do are: Task Management: The app provides a platform for users to create and organize their to-do lists. Users can add tasks, set deadlines, and categorize them based on priority or project.

Earnings on I-Do

Earning Potential: As users complete tasks on their to-do list, they earn monetary rewards. These rewards could accumulate in a virtual wallet within the app. Redemption Options: Users may have options for redeeming their earnings. They could cash out their earnings directly to their bank account, use them to purchase gift cards or products from partner merchants, or even donate them to charity.

Other Screens

On I-Do to-do App (Task management) users can keep track of their progress on daily tasks, earn their monetary rewards, schedule tasks for later, set reminders on each created task, sectionize their tasks into categories and set priorities to each unique tasks/to-dos upon creation
