Moving and relocation made convenient

Overview

OnTheMoove relocation platform serves as an online platform designed to assist individuals, families, Business owners who are planning to move to a new area, whether it's within the same city, across the country, or internationally. Here's an overview of what OnTheMoove relocation website offers:

Services Offered

All Round Relocations: On the moove provides comprehensive information about various destinations, including cities, neighborhoods, and suburbs. This information may include details about the cost of living, housing market, schools, healthcare facilities, transportation options, and relocations such as residential relocation, Corporate relocation, Store relocation, Event relocations and Other relocations.

Getting Quote

Users gets to request a relocation quote from the website. A quote contains information about the relocation, relocation destination and pick-up location, etc.

Other Screens

OnTheMoove provides the avenue for extra income that allows users to sign up to become drivers on the platform, and field agents.
